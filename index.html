About The Reset Grounds

The Reset Grounds is a farm-based transformation and leadership development company located in Brandenburg. We help executives and corporate teams step away from the digital world and reconnect with purpose, resilience, and authentic leadership.


Our programs combine regenerative agriculture, animal care, equine-assisted learning, physical farm work, coaching, and leadership development into one immersive experience.
