About The Reset Grounds

The Reset Grounds is a farm-based leadership and transformation company designed for professionals seeking renewal, resilience, and personal growth. Through regenerative agriculture, animal care, equine-assisted learning, nature immersion and executive coaching, participants gain practical leadership experience away from traditional corporate environments.
